Child Safety Standards

SaundVibe – Feel the Vibe

SaundVibe is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users, especially minors. We strictly prohibit any content that sexually exploits or endangers children.

Our Policy

  • SaundVibe has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content.
  • Any content depicting, promoting, or facilitating the sexual abuse or exploitation of minors is strictly prohibited.
  • Users who violate this policy will be immediately banned and reported to the appropriate authorities.
  • SaundVibe is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report child safety concerns directly within the SaundVibe app or by contacting us at:

support@saundvibe.app

Enforcement

  • All reported content is reviewed promptly.
  • Violating content is removed immediately upon verification.
  • Accounts involved in child safety violations are permanently banned.
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies in child safety investigations.

Contact

For child safety concerns, please contact us at:

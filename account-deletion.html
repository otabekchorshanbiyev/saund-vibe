Account Deletion Request

SaundVibe – Feel the Vibe

You can request the deletion of your SaundVibe account and all associated data by following the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the SaundVibe app on your device.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Tap "Account" section.
  4. Select "Delete Account".
  5. Confirm your identity and tap "Confirm Deletion".

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:
support@saundvibe.app

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  1. Your profile information (username, email, profile photo)
  2. All posts and vibes you have created
  3. Your saved library and resonance history
  4. Referral and earnings history
  5. All personal preferences and settings

What Data May Be Retained

⚠️ Some data may be retained for up to 90 days for legal and fraud prevention purposes, including transaction records required by payment processors. This data is not used for any other purpose and will be deleted after the retention period.

Partial Data Deletion

If you wish to delete specific data without deleting your account (such as individual posts or your saved library), you can do so directly within the app or by contacting us at support@saundvibe.app.

Contact Us

For any questions regarding account or data deletion, please reach out to us:

support@saundvibe.app
